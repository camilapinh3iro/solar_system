Astronaut

Astronauts' lives can be full of emotions, and sometimes even fun, but never easy. But what does an astronaut do in space, exactly? And what is the importance of the astronaut's work? Well, in addition to having to adapt to the low-gravity environment of space, they need to conduct and monitor various scientific experiments, in addition to carrying out spacewalks to maintain some equipment and even practice physical exercises to keep the body always healthy.
